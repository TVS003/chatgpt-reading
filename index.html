Created by Torrance Smith, educator and graduate student at Sam Houston.

Key Benefits of Using ChatGPT in Reading Education

Enhanced Comprehension

AI-powered tools help students understand complex texts and improve their reading skills.

Increased Engagement

Interactive AI conversations make learning more engaging and personalized for students.

Personalized Learning

Adaptive AI provides customized support based on individual student needs and learning styles.

About the Author

Hello, my name is Torrance Smith. I'm currently in the Curriculum and Instruction Master's program at Sam Houston State University. I teach reading and writing to third graders and am passionate about helping students become confident, reflective readers. My goal is to explore how AI tools like ChatGPT can support thoughtful, equitable, and engaging learning experiences in literacy instruction.
